About Surf Lessons with Argana Surf School

When you book a surf lesson, with Argana Surf School, you will experience a professional coaching from one of our ISA qualified coaches. For most of our students the first lesson is a life changing experience and it is just the beginning, so we offer lessons at all levels, from complete beginners and intermediates, through to advanced surf coaching.

All ages and abilities are welcome (though we ask that all children are at least 8 years old, and children aged 8-12 are supervised by an adult) and we are open all year.We provide surf lessons for individuals, families, small and large groups (including schools, unis surf clubs, stags & hen parties and corporate events).

At the Argana Surf School we are proud to specialise, not generalise. We teach surfing only, not kiteboarding, kayaking and so on!! This means we can concentrate on teaching surfing to the best level possible without distraction! Our coaches are Specialist Surf Coaches and qualified to the highest level.

We are the only Surf School in Newquay to boast not one, but two Ex-Professional Surfers on the Full Time Coaching Team: Head Coach Mike Young  & Senior Coach Reubin Pearce. Mike has been surfing for 35 years with 15 years professional experience on the World Tour. He was recently crowned British & English Champion in his division. Reubin has competed for over 10 years and in 2008 was the MA Pro Champion. We are proud of our instructors, all of which as well as being ISA qualified & Qualified Lifeguards, are all excellent surfers with years of experience in the ocean. We believe this is invaluable when it comes to giving you the knowledge and confidence to take to the water.

As well as offering Surf Lessons, we also offer a variety of packages which include surfing and accommodation from hostel based to luxury hotel!

Intermediate & Advanced Coaching

Our surf coaching covers all levels from complete beginners, through intermediate to advanced and technical surf coaching. We are the only surf school in Newquay, teaching advanced "outback" surfing for intermediate / advanced surfers

Kids School Holiday Surf Sessions

Aimed at children aged 8-13. Running every day from Monday to Friday throughout the school holidays (Easter to October) , we'll teach your kids how to surf in a safe and fun environment.

Teaching Methods

We offer a unique style of teaching, meaning all our clients will ride a wave unaided after only 1 lesson! And we teach "Outback" surfing to the highest level in Newquay. Our methods will enable quick progression whatever your ability
