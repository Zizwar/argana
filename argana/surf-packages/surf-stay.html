Surf & Stay at St Christopher's Inn

The Surf & Stay is our most popular surf & accommodation package. This is based at St Christopher’s Inn, which is one of Taghazout premier hostels. The location of the hostel is second to none, perched on the cliff with spectacular views overlooking Towan Beach and the whole of Taghazout Bay (and just above the surf school!)

The rooms sleep 4-10 shared dorm style and most are ensuite with sea views. For the girls there are also some all female dorms.

The Surf lessons are tailored to your current ability so whether you are a complete beginner, or are looking to improve your skills, then the Surf and Stay could be the package for you. Its a really popular deal for single travellers as well as groups.

The Surf & Stay package for any number of nights, most common is Friday - Monday, or even a full week: Saturday to Saturday, but it's completely up to you - even 1 nighters are possible!

We offer this package all year round; January to December. The price is subject to date, room choice and availability, but starts from £45 per person per night - low season.

All our Surf & Stay bookings include per night:

  • 1 x 2 hour Surf lesson with all equipment included (including winter wetsuits, boots, gloves and surfboard): all levels are taught*
  • A nights accommodation at St Christopher’s Inn: dorm rooms,  with sea view rooms
  • 25% discount off food at Belushis Bar & Restaurant

Booking is easy ONLINE  via the St Christophers website

Note: this link will take you to the St Christopher's inn, where you can book your Surf & Stay online by choosing the "Newquay" hostel in the dropdown. Alternatively, get in touch if you want more information

*Surf lessons are usually allocated for the day after your nights stay, although this can be flexible. We will be in touch to schedule your lessons, once your package is booked.

Winter Surf & Stay - from only £45* per person, per night

Taghazout is a great place to visit in the Winter season, the beaches and waves are quiet, the water isn’t that cold, the locals come out and the town reverts to the old fishing village vibe that it once had, with the surfers and hipsters thrown in the mix of course! If you are worried about the water temperature, there's no need, we have high quality 5mm Winter Wetsuits, Boots, Gloves & Hoods, as well as the best Hot Showers and Heated Changing Rooms.

Spring  & Autumn Surf & Stay - from only £55* per person, per night

The surf tends to be more consistent throughout Spring and Autumn, compared to Summer time, so it’s a great time of year to come and learn to surf or improve your surfing. The water is usually at its warmest in Autumn, and it's also less crowded than the Summer. Its our favourite time of year!

Summer Surf & Stay - from only £65* per person 

Summer is the most popular time to visit Newquay, so it can be very busy and the water can be crowded. If you like a really buzzy vibe, Summer sunset's from the beach and late nights, then Summer is the time for you! Some peak season / Summer dates do have a minimum 3 or 4 night stay.

*Price is subject to date selected, room choice and availability. 

*Surf lessons are usually allocated for the day after your nights stay, although this can be flexible.
