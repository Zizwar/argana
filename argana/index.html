Argana Surf School Taghazout Agadir

Surf Lessons In Taghazout - Learn to Surf with the Best!

Argana Surf School is based at the centre of MA surfing; Taghazout in Agadir and we've been teaching people to surf for over 18 years.

As well as being one of the longest running MA Surf Schools we are the only Surf School in Newquay to boast not one, but two Ex-Professional Surfers on their Full Time Coaching Team:  Head Coach Mike Young  & Senior Coach Reubin Pearce.

We love teaching complete beginners but we also teach Intermediate and Advanced Surf Coaching and are the only surf school in Taghazout offering outback, technical & progressive surf lessons on a daily basis.

We are a Specialist Surf School, in that we only teach Surfing. Our instructors are focused on their Surf Coaching, and not distracted by offering kayak lessons, coasteering, paddleboarding etc, so if you want to learn to surf or improve your surfing, with Specialist Surf Coaches then look no further.

We are open 12 months of the year, 7 days a week, closed only on Christmas Day, Boxing Day & New Years Day. Whatever the time of year, you can receive the best Surf Coaching from the experienced team at Argana Surf School.

Book Online!

It's super quick & easy to book your surf lessons via our online booking system. Choose your lesson types, pick your dates & time, fill in your details & pay via credit / debit card and receive instant confirmation! EASY!

Private Surf Lessons

Private Lessons are a really popular choice for many of our clients. If you want to recieve intensive professional surf coaching to really accelerate your learning curve, then Private lessons are for you!

Group Surf Lessons and Courses

Argana Surf School offer a variety of Group Surf lesson packages. From a one day taster session , to a week long intensive course, we offer a Surf Lesson course to suit your needs.

 

Intermediate Surf Lessons

We are the only Taghazout Surf School to offer regular "outback" progressive lessons, where we teach our clients, the more technical aspects of intermediate and advanced levels surfing.

Surf & Stay

Join us for the ultimate Surf & Stay - from only £45 per person, per night.

Each night booked includes 1 x 2 hour surf lesson (with all equipment included), overnight accommodation in sea-view rooms & 25% off food at Belushis.

Video Analysis

If you are serious about improving your surfing, video analysis is an excellent tool to enable you to progress. We currently offer 2 options for Video Analysis:  Private Lesson + Video Analysis  OR  Remote Video Analysis

 

Surf Lesson Prices

With prices starting from GBP30 per lesson (for 6 lessons or more), for 2 hours with all equipment included, surf lessons with Escape is money well spent! We teach all abilities, so you'll find a package to suit your needs.

 

Large Group Bookings

Our facilties, experienced instructors and unique coaching methods, mean we can offer great surf lessons for larger groups. We can host school groups, surf clubs, corporate days and of course, stag and hen groups!

Enhanced Surf Performance Weekend

A weekend of Technical Surf Coaching, with Video Analysis, Surf Specific Training and Yoga, aimed at Intermediate Surfers. Dates now released for Spring 2023. Limited Space -  Book Now!

Kids School Holiday Surf Sessions

Aimed at children aged 8-13. Running every day from Monday to Friday throughout the school holidays (Easter to October) , we'll teach your kids how to surf in a safe and fun environment.

Gift Vouchers

Gift Vouchers make an Ideal Gift for the Surfer (or wannabe surfer) in your Life! All our Lesson packages are available as Gift Vouchers, and will be posted to you, or the recipient of your choosing!
