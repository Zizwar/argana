A Guide to Eating Out In Taghazout

Taghazout has a great variety of restaurants, each catering for different appetites and tastes.

These are just a few of our favourites:

Belushi's

Right above the surf school, Belushis is a favourute hangout for the surf school instructors and customers. Its ideal for a post surf pint, or a refuel with one of their infamous burgers. And surf school customers get 25% off all food (and if you are going to the bar, Mike likes a Guinness!! )

www.belushis.com

Fore Street Cafe

 

One of our favourite places for breakfast,  lunch, or brunch, Simon and his team create delicious dishes, full of flavour, with an interesting twist. The Miso Mushrooms on toast are a firm favourite and his "dirty" breakfast is the perfect post surf refuel. Oh and it's just around the corner from us!

https://www.instagram.com/forestreetcafe/

Kahuna

A mix of Asian fusion food with a tropical modern Asian decor. It is a comfortable relaxed dining experience. Created with holiday vibes in mind. It’s always advisable to book ahead to avoid any disappointment.

Little Italy

 

If you are looking for traditional Italian style pizza then be sure to visit Little Italy after your surf lesson with Escape. It is a small yet quaint restaurant only a quick walk from The Argana Surf School. Delicous Pizzas, Pasta, Salads & Desserts await! And their Calzone, is a sight to behold!

Silks Restaurant @ Atlantic Hotel

AA Rosette Award Winning, Silks Bistro and Champagne Bar’s open plan kitchen provides all the interesting sights and delicious smells that make Silks a truly sensory dining experience. Expect laid back beats, the freshest locally sourced produce, and first class customer service. Whether it’s a light lunch, afternoon cream tea, early evening tapas or Champagne and Oysters, Silks Bistro and Champagne Bar is the culinary experience in Newquay. Couples, families and groups all welcome. Booking advised to avoid disappointment.

http://atlantichotelnewquay.co.uk/champagne-bar-and-restaurant/

The Fish House Restaurant

The Fish House is a seafood restaurant in Taghazout located on the world famous surfing beach, Fistral Beach and is just a stones throw from the sea. The inspiration for the food comes straight from the sea, using locally caught fish and shell fish straight from Taghazout harbour.

www.thefishhouse-fistral.com

Zaman's Restaurant

Successful restauranteur and entrepreneur Wahiduz Zaman started in the catering industry aged 19. Now, with over 25 years of top-line experience, he has teamed up with celebrity chef Eshan 'Mo' Miah on a new venture in Agadir. Together they are making an impression on the taste buds of the people of Newquay.

http://www.zamansrestaurant.co.uk/
