Escape to Portugal 2023

SOLD OUT!!!!!

Join Us on An Exclusive Intermediate Surf Coaching Trip to Southern Portugal

DATES: Sunday 26th March 2023 - Saturday 1st April 2023

Price: £1049 per person (based on shared twin or triple rooms)

Overview:

This trip will include 5 full days of Surf Coaching with Mike Young & Reubin Pearce to include: surf guiding (travel to best beach for conditions), intermediate surf coaching, video analysis and lots of good times!

We will be based at a Villa with plunge pool,  located on the outskirts of the Aljezur Region (which is about 80 minutes drive from Faro Airport and a 5 minute drive / 20 minute walk from Arrifana Beach)

Transfers to & from Faro airport are included.
4 evening meals are included, and breakfast & lunch on all 6 days.

Accommodation:

You can view the accommodation here: https://www.arrifanasurflodge.com/accommodation/
The guest accommodation is across 4 x twin / triple rooms, which unless specified otherwise will be allocated based on gender. The trip will be limited to max 8 guests only, therefore ideal allocation is 2 people per room.

Unfortunately there are only 4 rooms + a bunk room at the accommodation, so no private rooms are available.

  • Shared lounge & kitchen facilities
  • Plunge pool
  • Wifi throughout
  • Bedding & towel provided
  • Terrace with hammocks & sea view
  • Firepit & BBQ

Food & Drink

Continental breakfast is served daily and includes a selection of cereals, fresh milk, yogurt, fresh bread, jams, honey, homemade peanut butter, ham, cheese, fresh fruit & eggs (anyway you like).

Lunch is provided every surf day. A lunch buffet table will be set up and guests get to fill their own lunchbox with a choice of  fillings which include: hummus, chicken mayo, tuna salad, salad greens, pasta, fresh rolls and much more.

4 x Evening Meals are provided on Sunday, Tuesday, Wednesday, & Thursday. 2 of these meals will be a 3 course dinner at the lodge. The other 2 evenings will be meals at a couple of local restaurants (the cost of this is included). For the other 2 meals, we can either choose to eat out again, or cook / BBQ at the lodge (cost of these 2 meals is not included)

Dietary requirements can be catered for.

Also included:

  • All Soft Drinks
  • Unlimited tea, (herbal & PG tips) fresh coffee, & hot chocolate
  • Loads of fresh fruit, light snacks, and beverages are provided throughout the day

Surf Guiding & Coaching

This package is invited guests only, so we will know the surfing levels of all our guests. Obviously there may be some differences in abilities & experience, but all guests will be "intermediate" surfers, able to catch their own waves unaided.

We will aim for 2 surfs per day, both will be coached by Mike & Reubin. The location and chosen surf spot will be decided based on your abilities and conditions - we either have the choice of West facing spots, or South facing (good if the swell is big). We will aim to accommodate all levels, and go out in conditions that will allow all to learn, progress and challenge yourself.

Transport to & from the chosen surf break is included - we have the use of 2 x 9 seaters, with drivers, so plenty of room for boards etc.

We will film some sessions, from the water / land which can be analysed "informally" during the week. 

Surfboards & Wetsuits are not included in the price, however you have the choice of:

  • Bringing your own board
  • Renting from the lodge or other Surf shop (there are quite a few in the area)
  • Renting from Escape and transporting the board yourself

About the Area

Our accommodation is nestled in the quite village of Vale Da Telha in the Costa Vincenta National Park located less than 5 minutes drive from Arrifana Beach and only 20 minutes on foot! We are less than 6 kilometres away from the charming white-washed hillside town of Aljezur.

Located on the southwest coast of Portugal, the area receives a consistent year round Atlantic swell, making it one of the best places in the world for surfing. There are lots of sandy beaches on the south facing coastline, which are good when the swell is is big,  as well as west facing point breaks and reef breaks for the more experienced surfing crowd to be explored.

Mike & Reubin have explored the area already so know some of the good places to surf. We will also have the services of local guides with transport (2 x 9 seater vans)

In the Spring the air temperatures are 17 - 22ºC, with mild winds and usually sunny days. The water temperature in March / April is 15-19 ºC so a 4/3 wetsuit (or good 3/2) would be suitable.

Dates:

Accommodation: 6 nights: Sunday 26th March - Saturday 1st April 2023.

Coaching Days: 27th - 31st March 2023 (5 full days)

Travel Days: 26th March & 1st April 2023 - we recommend you travel on these dates, to ensure you benefit from the 5 full days of coaching.  Transfers from & to Faro Airport on these dates are included in the price (as long as we know your flight arrival times).

If your departure airport restricts the day you can fly out to Saturday 25th, then there is an option for an extra night at the accommodation, however, please note the surf coaching & guiding will not commence until the Monday 27th.

What’s Not Included:

  • Flights (and additional baggage)
  • Additional food / drinks - to include 2 evening meals and any wines, beers, soft drinks etc
  • Travel Insurance
  • Surf boards / Wetsuits 
  • Transfers outside of the dates above
