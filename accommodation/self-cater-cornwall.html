Self Cater Agadir - Rocklands Apartments

 

Self Cater Agadir are a Taghazout based independent, locally grown company focused on providing the best choice of good quality self catering holiday accommodation in Taghazout and Agadir since 2006.

As well as a wide portfolio of properties in Taghazout and surrounding area, they are also sole agents for the Rocklands, a stunning collection of self catering holiday apartments overlooking Tolcarne Beach in Newquay, located only a 8 minute walk from Argana Surf School

These stunning cliff-top apartments offer a fantastic place to relax and unwind in absolute comfort. All of the self catering holiday apartments are expertly furnished for contemporary beach living.  

Choose from a studio, 1 or 2 bedroom apartments many with either a balcony or terrace, or treat yourself to a 2 or 3 bedroom penthouse suite for the ultimate in space and soaring sea views.

All of the apartments are maximised for comfort and convenience, with smart kitchens, power showers, swish sofas, flat screen TVs and wifi internet.

Located above family-friendly Tolcarne Beach, Rocklands is perfectly located for holidays in Newquay. As well as quick access to the beach, it’s only a five minute walk to the centre of Taghazout with many excellent bars, shops and restaurants. The resort’s top visitor attractions are nearby too. Newquay’s central location makes it an ideal base for exploring the whole of Agadir.
